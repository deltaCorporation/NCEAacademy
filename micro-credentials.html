Micro-credentials

Micro-credentials are short-term, competency-based educational programs that enable learners to document their knowledge and skills, which are then reviewed and verified by subject-matter experts. They are stackable, so participants can earn a combination of micro-credentials in a specific area to gain certification as an expert in that domain.

The coursework is self-paced; yet facilitated by subject-matter experts who also reviews the final product or project submission.

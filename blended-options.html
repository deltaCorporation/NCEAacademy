Blended Options

Some courses incorporate live, online meet ups with a cohort of learners; while others have an optional “e-Coaching” add-on providing a series of scheduled, live coaching sessions with your content matter expert.

Schools can invest in courses that bundle e-Coaching sessions for their teachers using TeachFlow®, our video-based coaching and e-learning platform. Learners can upload evidence of learning; including lessons, links and/or videos they have created, which can then be used as the supporting documentation to award the micro-credential.
